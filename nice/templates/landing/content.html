Your Calendar, Reimagined

ReCal is the calendar designed just for you.

Your Agenda, Recalled

Princeton students do not have the time to hunt for things in their calendars. We know that, which is why we came up with the agenda view. Decide what is important to you - lectures, precepts, assignments, etc. - , and we will show you just those items in your agenda.

Editing is a breeze

Every part of ReCal has been designed to work just right. See an error in an event? To edit, just click on the error, and type away.

Socially Engineered

Noticed that an event for a class is missing from ReCal? Adding it is a click away - and your friends get to benefit, too.

Position your events, your way

ReCal is your calendar - it will look exactly the way you want. Want to see two events side by side? No problem, just drag, and you are good to go.

Looks great in black

Speaking of looks, there is a dark theme, too. Choose whichever fits you more.
