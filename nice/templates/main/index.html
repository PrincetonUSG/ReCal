Getting Started with ReCal

Here's all you need to know to get started:

Click any event in Agenda or Calendar view to see its details.
You can drag and drop the details popup anywhere.
It will even snap to the sidebar. Just drag it towards the right side of the page.

Done with an assignment, or never want to go to lecture again?
Click the Hide button (half-closed eye icon) at the top of the event popup.
If you want to get it back, click Settings at the top right and enable Hidden Events. Click the eye button again to unhide.

In the event popup, click any field to edit. Your edits will be highlighted in yellow.
Don't forget to click the Save button at the top of the popup to save your changes!

Start a new event by clicking the Add Event button at the top right of the page. Don't worry, we'll warn you if someone has already added the event you're trying to create.

At the top right, click the Edit Profile link to change the classes you're signed up for.
You can change your display name here, too!

Last piece of advice to get you up and rolling:
If you see a "There are unapproved changes" popup, click it to see new changes that other users have submitted.
Vote unapproved changes up or down — we'll reward you with points for your votes!
Your reward's going to be especially big if you vote with the hive-mind...

Thanks for joining ReCal!
